Welcome to Mabini

Mabini

Mabini, a picturesque municipality located on the western coast of Batangas, is a paradise for beach lovers and diving enthusiasts. Known for its stunning beaches and crystal-clear waters, the area boasts numerous dive sites, including the famous Sepoc Beach and the vibrant coral gardens of Batangas Bay, attracting divers from around the world to explore its rich marine biodiversity.

Beyond its aquatic allure, Mabini is also steeped in history and culture. Visitors can explore historical sites like the San Miguel Arcangel Parish Church, which showcases beautiful architecture and local traditions. The town is also known for its annual festivals, such as the Pista ng Bayan, which celebrates local culture through vibrant parades and traditional performances.
