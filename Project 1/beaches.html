Beaches

Beaches

Mabini, Batangas is a popular destination for diving and snorkeling, particularly in the Anilao area. While the town of Mabini isn't directly on the sea, Anilao, a part of the municipality, is renowned for its underwater attractions. It's famous for its diverse marine life and coral reefs, making it a favorite among divers and snorkelers.

Aguila Beach Resort is a place to invigorate your tired body and mind. You can enjoy the waters of Anilao beach, walk along the coast, or swim, snorkel or scuba dive. The resort is also suitable for business meetings and seminars, and as a romantic getaway for newlyweds.

Maricaban Island, part of the island municipality of Tingloy in Batangas, Philippines, is known for its beautiful beaches and clear waters, making it a popular destination for beach lovers and divers. It's relatively close to Manila, making it an accessible escape.
