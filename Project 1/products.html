Local Products & Delicacies

Mabini, Batangas is a great place to enjoy Batangas' local cuisine. Some popular dishes to try include Batangas Lomi, Bulalo, and Sinaing na Tulingan. Mabini is also known for its diving and snorkeling, so you might find seafood restaurants that offer fresh catches.

Kapeng Barako, also known as Liberica coffee, is a unique and flavorful coffee varietal native to the Philippines. It's characterized by its distinctive aroma, often described as floral and fruity, and its rich, slightly smoky flavor. While once a dominant coffee in the Philippines, it's now a rare and cherished coffee, with Batangas and Cavite being the main production areas.

Tableya is a traditional Filipino chocolate tablets produced in the municipality of Mabini, Batangas. Tableya (also spelled "tablea") is a pure, unsweetened chocolate product made from roasted and ground cacao beans, typically molded into small discs or balls.
